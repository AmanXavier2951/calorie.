Understanding BMI and BMR

BMI (Body Mass Index)

BMI is a measure of body fat based on your height and weight. It helps to assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)

BMR is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic physiological functions like breathing and digestion while at rest. It is a key component in determining your daily calorie requirements.
